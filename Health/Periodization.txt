@ Periodization

Discerning when and how and why to change exercise programming.


## Why

Stimuli need to change when you hit a plateau to continue triggering adaptations.

SAID ~ Specific Adaptation to Imposed Demands


## How

Choice [of exercise]
Order [of exercise]
Frequency [of exercising]
Intensity [of exercises]
Volume [of exercise]
Rest [intervals in/around exercise]


## When

Every 3-9 months
- It could be seasonal
  - Fits naturally
  - Keeps it interesting, allows for updates
- On/Off Season
  - Fairly normal within the community
