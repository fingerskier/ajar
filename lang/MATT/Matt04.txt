1.
Tote 'o 'Isous 'anyxthy eis tyn 'erymon 'ypo tou pneumatos,
Then this Jesus lead  to the wilderness under the spirit,
peirasthynai 'upo tou diabolou.
temptations under the devil.

kai nysteusas 'ymeras tesserakonta kai nuktas tesserakonta 'usteron 'epeinasen.

3.
kai proselthwn 'i peirazwn eipen   autow  Ei uios ei tou theou,

eipe 'ina oi lithoi outoi 'artoi genwntai.
speak to the rocks those bread become
4.

'o 'anthrwpos, 'all 'epi panti 'rymati 'ekporeuomenw dia stomatos theou.

kai 'estysen auton 'epi to pterugion tou ieou,
