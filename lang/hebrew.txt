Vowels follow the letter
  A_     ah    patach
  A_|    aah   qamats
  A|_    o     hataph-qamats

  A.     ee    hiriq
  A..    ay    tsere
  A...   ehh   segol

  Ao     oh    holam
  A-     ooh   shuruq

  A|     x/e   shva  
  
====

Pronouns
  1s  ANC' / AN'    he/she
  2s  A_T_|H        you
  3s  HVoA          they/them


prefixes
  B ~ in/with/by
    B_| ~ in
    B_ ~ in the
  C ~ as/like
  H ~ the
  M ~ from/out-of
  V ~ and
  ` ~ to
  L ~ for/to

  conjugations
    A ~ I
    ' ~ He
    N ~ We
    T ~ She
  
  others
    H ~ interrogative
    $ ~ that/which/whom
    V ~ past <-> future


suffixes
  ' ~ I/mine
  D ~ you/thee
  H ~ his/hers
  'HM ~ theirs
  'N ~ his/hers/theirs
  N ~ paragogic/emphasis
  V ~ his/hers
