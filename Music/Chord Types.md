# Nomenclature 

* 1st, root ~ changing the root changes the chord
* 3rd, suspension ~ b2, 2, 3, 4
* 5th, dominant ~ b5, 5, #5
* 7th, lead ~ 6, b7, 7
* iii, inversion ~ the makeup of the triad (lead tone is always above)
