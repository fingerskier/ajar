The Guitarists' Glossary
  not that these terms are unique to guitaring but the definitions are specified
  for guitarists.


chord progression

homophony - one dominant melodic voice accompanied by chords.

intonation - the quality of a note.  This refers to both pitch and aesthetics.  This is also used to refer to the correctness of the pitch on a string at every fret.

monophony - a singley-voiced melody.

polyphony - two or more simultaneous, independent melodies.
